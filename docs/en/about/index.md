<section class="about-container">
  <h2>💫 Origin</h2>
  <p>
    <strong>Mengyue Components</strong> has no team, no funding, and no predefined roadmap.
    It began as a personal journey—a developer's exploration to build a modern component system from scratch.
  </p>

  <p>
    Every component, animation, and line of logic was meticulously crafted during late-night hours.
    From architectural considerations to interaction design, from styling details to performance optimization,
    each iteration represents a milestone in personal growth.
    While some components may still have room for improvement, continuous refinement ensures every feature and API evolves toward perfection.
  </p>

  <h2>🧩 Philosophy</h2>
  <p>
    This component library isn't about showcasing technical prowess, but rather finding an elegant balance—
    <em>bringing order to code, naturalness to interactions, and lightness to development.</em>
  </p>

  <p>
    Therefore, significant time has been dedicated to contemplating:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>How to make component logic more concise and reusable?</li>
    <li>How to create a styling system that's both flexible and consistent?</li>
    <li>How to minimize bundle size and accelerate loading performance?</li>
    <li>How to make documentation more intuitive and accessible?</li>
    <li>How to maintain simplicity while ensuring universal applicability across diverse needs?</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>🚀 From 0 to 1</h2>
  <p>
    From the very first button to a complete build system with automated deployment pipelines,
    every detail of <strong>Mengyue Components</strong> embodies thoughtful practice and reflection.
  </p>

  <p>
    Without team collaboration, UI designers, or dedicated QA engineers,
    all aspects—including design, coding, documentation, testing, bundling, and publishing—were accomplished independently.
    This journey represents not just technical exploration, but also a testament to patience, aesthetic sensibility, and genuine passion.
  </p>

  <h2>🌈 Vision</h2>
  <p>
    While there are undoubtedly areas for improvement, <strong>Mengyue Components</strong> will continue evolving.
    We hope it becomes a starting point for developers to learn from, draw inspiration from, and eventually contribute to—
    growing through community engagement and maturing through collective experience.
  </p>

  <p class="thanks">
    Finally, thank you for every click and visit.
  </p>
</section>
